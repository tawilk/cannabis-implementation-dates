# US Cannabis Policy & Retail Implementation Dates

Up-to-date list of each US state &amp; DC for cannabis policy implementation and retail access for medical and recreational (adult-use) cannabis (marijuana)

This repository provides a harmonized dataset of medical and adult-use cannabis policy and retail implementation dates for all 50 U.S. states and the District of Columbia.
The dataset was developed to improve consistency in health policy evaluations by distinguishing statutory legalization from the date legal retail access first became available.

# Variables
Variable	Description

state           Name of State

medical	          First year of legal medical retail sale

recreational      First year legal adult-use retail sale

mml_effect        Date of medical cannabis policy in effect

mml_retail        Date of first legal medical retail sale

rml_effect        Date of adult-use cannabis policy in effect

rml_retail        Date of fist legal adult-use retail sale

mml_retail_cite   Citation of first medical retail date

rml_retail_cite   Citation of first adult-use retail date

mml_cite_act      Act/bill/policy legalizing medical cannabis use

rml_cite_act      Act/bill/policy legalizign adult-use cannabis use
